Our Team

They are qualified, understand child development and the teaching methodology. This allows them to promote children's social and cognitive aptitude.

Our staff are trained to engage children in meaningful conversations, expand their knowledge and vocabulary, use open-ended questioning, and encourage problem-solving skills. They teach important concepts through activities, everyday experiences, collaborative assignments, and active curriculum.

Teachers prepare activities that reinforce the curriculum based on a specific need or theme of the week.

They regularly assess each child's progress and adjust as necessary. The assessments are purposeful, developmentally appropriate and take place in the natural setting for planning and to guide instruction.

All our teachers receive a minimum of 10 internal in-service professional development sessions each year.

We also have high-quality interns and energetic work study students who serve as teacher assistants, they are given adequate experience to progress in the field of education.
