Six C’s to Success

A place of exploration, independence, and challenge.

Kidz Kollege is built upon the six Cs of Success which are the founding principles that pave the path and provide inspiration in our journey to cultivate the children's talent. The Six Cs are the heart of Kidz Kollege educational framework that brings together dynamic leadership, a compelling curriculum, engaging instruction, continuous improvement, and a positive school culture.

Creativity

Since this is the essence of learning. The creativity and originality of our children is constantly encouraged through our individualized approach to student expression and the multiple resources they use.

Connecting minds

The open collaboration and communication at Kidz kollege creates an educational atmosphere that serves to connect the hearts, minds, and ideas for our children to thrive in our multicultural environment.

Critical thinking

Through the promotion of independent thinking, personal autonomy, problem solving and reasoning, children learn critical thinking skills that allow them to analyze facts and opinions. This is an important life skills that children learn at Kidz Kollege.

Communication

We believe that open channels of communication between children, teachers, management, and parents are essential as it is an integral component of our school success.

Collaboration

At Kidz Kollege, collaboration is the building block of our educational system where diversity of intellects is celebrated, uniqueness is appreciated and the talents are valuated through collaboration academic activities, team work, and sharing responsibility to achieve a common goal.

Community

We share a common vision at the heart of providing an educational experience that extends far beyond the boundaries of the school. Community building in and outside of the school through friendly relationships, educational events and parental involvement is a corner-stone of Kidz Kollege.
