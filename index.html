Welcome to Kidz  Kollege

At Kidz Kollege, we strive for excellence in learning, but we also aim to develop the personal qualities that will allow our children to master confidence, curiosity and a growth mindset that is required to become independent lifelong learners. We therefore take full pride and responsibility to ensure that our children receive an outstanding quality education and are taught good values that will enable them to bloom into fruitful individuals. We foster an atmosphere that promotes creative capacities within children and help them utilize their full potential while instilling a "love for learning". The school is committed to offering an opportunity to develop through an engaging and integrated challenging curriculum.
